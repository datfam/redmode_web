RedMode respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

RedMode is designed with a privacy-first approach. Data is stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud.

We do not sell or share your personal data. The only data transmitted externally is your chat message content and current cycle phase, sent to Groq for processing.

Groq processes data in accordance with Groq's Privacy Policy and provides equivalent data protection through encrypted HTTPS transmission and by not retaining conversation content.

  • iCloud data encrypted by Apple
  • Groq communication via HTTPS

  • Turn off notifications in iOS Settings
