Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 24, 2026
RedMode ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1 Information We Collect

RedMode is designed with a privacy-first approach. Cycle data, notes, anniversaries, and Vault information are stored locally on your device and synced via your personal iCloud. We cannot access this data.

  • Local data: Cycle information, notes, vault, settings ‚Äî stored on your device and your personal iCloud
  • Subscription data: If you purchase PRO, transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect payment information
  • Bro AI data: When using the AI chat feature, your questions are sent to a third-party AI provider (Groq) for processing. We do not store chat history on our servers
  • Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools

2 How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized cycle forecasts and advice
  • Process AI chat requests (via third-party provider)
  • Manage PRO subscriptions (via Apple)

3 Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third parties. The only data transmitted externally is AI chat content sent to Groq for processing, which is deleted after the response is generated.

4 Data Security

The Vault is protected by biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID). Locally stored data and iCloud synced data are encrypted by Apple.

5 Your Rights

  • Delete all data at any time from the app's Settings
  • Disable iCloud sync to keep data on-device only
  • Turn off notifications at any time

6 Children's Privacy

RedMode is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7 Policy Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

8 Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact: redmode.app@gmail.com
